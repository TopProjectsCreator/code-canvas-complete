## Great feedback makes projects feel professional

Sound and visual reactions help users understand what just happened — success, warning, failure, or progress.

## Minimum effects pack (recommended)

- Click/tap sound
- Error alert sound
- Success chime
- Small animation on key actions
- Completion celebration

## Keep effects friendly and accessible

- Add mute toggle
- Keep default volume moderate
- Avoid rapid flashing
- Do not block interaction with long animations

## Where to place feedback

- **Buttons:** instant confirmation
- **Form submit:** success/error state
- **Level complete:** celebration cue
- **Warnings:** subtle but clear alert

## QA checklist before sharing

- Triggered once per event
- Volume balanced across effects
- No overlapping chaotic sounds
- Works on both mobile and desktop
